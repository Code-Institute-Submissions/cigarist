About

Welcome

to Cigarist. A place where cigar people like yourself can post up your own cigars with tasting notes and also see what everyone else is smoking in the world of cigars. It is very simple to get started. Get yourself registered with us by selecting a unique username and a password and you are on your way. Once you are registerd just login and start posting away. If you are anything like us at Cigarist we constantly wonder about the taste of a cigar. For instance when you are smoking a lovely Cuban Montecristo No.02 and you get those notes of light spice and pepper blending effortlessly with notes of almonds and cedar. Do you just wonder if another person smoking that same cigar gets the same flavour profiles? From our own experience we can say sometimes you do. However there are definitely times that we do not get the same flavours and it is always intersting to know what someone else tastes. Even simpler you may want to smoke some new cigars and wonder what might be good for you. Here at Cigarist you will be able to search a multitude of posts and see what cigars you might be thinking of trying and get the tasting notes from other users as well.
